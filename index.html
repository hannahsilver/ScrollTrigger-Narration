My name is Rachael Popowich, I am a free will astrologer based out of Montreal. My style and service is aimed at using natal astrology as a psychosocial tool.

Often, when grappling with a personal crisis clinical language is helpful. But what if descriptions of that nature don’t speak to us deeply enough? The astrological alphabet of shapes and symbols, when interpreted thoughtfully and with care can offer an antidote to simple reductions of personality.

Isn’t there a more mysterious and soulful vocabulary that can mirror the mysterious and soulful complexity of your experience?

Astrology is a strange language that has the capacity to do that. That strangeness is its power.

Clock time can delineate a minute, but astrology can delineate the quality of a moment. Often we engage with the concept of time by observing the effects of it, astrological language is interested in the quality of it as well. What a refreshment for the soul, to be able to occasionally converse intimately about the occult operations of ourselves.

I see my natal chart readings as a “You Are Here” arrow on the map of meaning. Making sense of where you are, where you come from, and where you are going. It is immensely powerful to gain insight into the narrative arch of one’s life. The intention of my service is to do exactly that.

If this interests you and you have any further questions about the philosophy or pragmatics of my practice, please ask your questions via the contact email provided. It would be my pleasure and privilege to work with you.

With Love. Rachael.
